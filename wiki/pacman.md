## Pacman++

These are a few pacman options worth checking in `/etc/pacman.conf`:

##### Colored output:
Uncomment `#Color` in the `# Misc options` section for a colorful pacman experience.

##### Waka waka waka waka:
Uncomment `#ILoveCandy` in the `# Misc options` section.
